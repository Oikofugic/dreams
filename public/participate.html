Participate in this project!

Would you like to share a piece of your subconscious with the collective? In time, I hope to add functionality for you to record or upload files here. But for now, if you'd like to add your voice or vision to this project, I welcome you to:

* Record a dream you had as an mp3, wav or aiff file.
* Take a dreamy digital or analog photograph (and/or make a gif).
* Email your audio or visual files to me at info @ wanderingarrow.com

Alternately, if you live in NYC, we can arrange to meet and I can record your dream. You can reach me at info @ wanderingarrow.com
